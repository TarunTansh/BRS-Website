Bharat Rojgar Sewa (BRS) — Terms and Conditions

Last Updated: 01 November 2025

1. Purpose

Bharat Rojgar Sewa is an online employment facilitation platform that connects job seekers and employers by sharing relevant contact details. It enables digital registration, job search, and listing through the website or WhatsApp. BRS does not conduct background checks, verification, or any direct communication between job seekers and employers. The communication that follows is solely between the two parties.

2. Eligibility

  • Users must be at least 18 years old or have parental/guardian consent to register.
  • By using BRS, you confirm that the information you provide is true, accurate, and complete.
  • BRS reserves the right to limit or discontinue access to users found providing false or misleading information.

3. User Accounts

To access certain features, you may need to create an account using your mobile number, email, or social login. You are responsible for maintaining confidentiality of your login credentials. Any activity carried out from your account or WhatsApp number linked to BRS will be treated as your responsibility.

4. Services Offered

  • Candidate registration and job listing
  • Employer job posting and candidate browsing
  • Sharing of contact details between employers and candidates for independent communication

Payments must be made only through official payment gateways, UPI intent links, or authorized BRS WhatsApp payment options. All payments are non-refundable unless explicitly stated otherwise.

6. No Guarantee Clause

We do not guarantee job joining or retention. We only guarantee lead generation services.
